About IGTC

The Intercontinental GT Challenge represents the pinnacle of global GT3 endurance racing. Organized by SRO Motorsports Group in collaboration with the FIA, IGTC brings together the world's most prestigious endurance events in a compact, high-intensity format.

Our unique "One Month Season" condenses the world's most challenging endurance races into a single month of non-stop action, testing teams, drivers, and machinery to their absolute limits.

IGTC showcases the ultimate combination of speed, strategy, and endurance across six iconic circuits spanning four continents.
